What is Artificial Intellegance?

AI explores creating new realities (images, music) & understanding existing ones (image translation, editing). Buckle up, the future's getting visual and interactive!

How does Machine Learning relate to Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the broad field of creating intelligent machines, while machine learning (ML) is a specific technique within AI. Think of AI as the overall goal and machine learning as one of the tools used to achieve it.

Is Artificial Intelligence replacing human jobs?

AI automates tasks, transforming jobs (think robots & data entry) but also creates new ones (think AI developers & robot trainers). Get ready to adapt!

What are the different types of Artificial Intelligence?

Today's AI excels at specific tasks (facial recognition, chess whizz), but future AI dreams of human-like learning and adaptability.
